DIA: Floor Narrowed, Swing Pressure Still Overhead

Session date: 2026-03-27 | Session time (ET): 09:30 to 15:59

Returning to DIA at the close, the floor had narrowed from the earlier read. Order-Flow Pressure was the main support source underneath, with Trend still helping hold the session together and Money Flow adding only a minimal stabilizing contribution. Overhead, Swing Move was the clearest strain source by a wide margin, while Volume Shock and Volatility Pulse also leaned against the tape, and the immediate move sat only slightly on the pressure side.

That matters because DIA did not carry its earlier broader floor all the way into the bell. The tape was still being held from underneath by flow, but the support shelf was narrower by the end, while the broader swing and participation stress remained overhead. In plain English, DIA closed under pressure, but not on a totally absent floor.
