QQQ: Broad Floor, Volume Pressure Overhead

Session date: 2026-03-27 | Session time (ET): 09:30 to 15:59

At the close, QQQ finished with a broader floor than a simple one-way unwind. Short Move was the main support source underneath, with Order-Flow Pressure also helping hold the session together. Trend and Volatility Pulse added smaller support contributions. Overhead, Volume Shock was the clearest strain source, with Swing Move and Money Flow also leaning against the tape.

That matters because QQQ did not close on a thin shelf alone. The floor was still doing real work underneath, especially through the immediate move and order-flow support. But the session also never cleared the overhead burden, and the close still carried a real participation-and-swing pressure stack. In plain English, QQQ finished held together, but not comfortable.
