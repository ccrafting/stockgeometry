QQQ: Supported Underneath, Pressured Overhead

Session date: 2026-03-25 | Session time (ET): 09:30 to 12:54

QQQ has already narrowed into one main session shape, but that shape is not one-way. Order-flow pressure and trend are doing real work to hold the tape together, with the immediate move and money flow also leaning to the support side. At the same time, volatility pulse, swing pressure, and volume shock are still pressing from above.

That leaves QQQ looking organized, but internally contested. This is not a clean, easy advance, and it is not a thinly supported one either. It is a growth-heavy tape with a real floor underneath it, but with enough volatility and participation stress overhead to keep the session tight rather than relaxed.

Relative to the earlier SPY read today, QQQ looks less thinly supported and more evenly contested. SPY looked pressure-led on a narrow support shelf; QQQ still has pressure overhead, but it also has a stronger underlying floor.
