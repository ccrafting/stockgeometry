SPY: Held Together, Never Relaxed

Session date: 2026-03-23 | Session time (ET): 09:30 to 14:32

By early afternoon, SPY was still holding shape, but the balance had already tilted into strain. Volatility Pulse led the pressure side, Volume Shock reinforced it, and support narrowed toward Order-Flow Pressure and Short Move rather than a broad, easy base.

That matters because this session was still organized, just no longer comfortable. The tape was being held together by a real support shelf underneath, but the stress stack was widening overhead, so this reads as containment under load rather than a settled bullish structure.
