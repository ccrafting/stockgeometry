Session date: 2026-03-25 | Session time (ET): 09:30 to 15:19

Returning to QQQ later in the session, the floor is still there, but it is narrower than it was earlier. Order-Flow Pressure and Trend are still doing real work underneath, with only a small contribution from the immediate move. But Swing Move, Volume Shock, Volatility Pulse, and now Money Flow are all leaning to the pressure side.

That matters because the tape is still organized, but less balanced than it looked earlier in the day. The session is holding shape, yet the support shelf has thinned while the overhead strain stack has widened. In plain English: QQQ is still intact, but it has shifted toward a more pressure-led afternoon profile.

Relative to the later SPY read, which grew a firmer floor into the afternoon, QQQ moved the other way: its support narrowed and Money Flow crossed onto the pressure side. That leaves QQQ as the more burdened side of the tape, even though it is still not disorderly.
