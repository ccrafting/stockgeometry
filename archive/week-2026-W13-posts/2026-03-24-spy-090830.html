SPY: Support-Led, Still Taking Shape

Session date: 2026-03-24 | Session time (ET): 09:30 to 12:06

SPY is narrowing into one shape rather than rotating through multiple competing ones. Under the surface, support is broad: trend, money flow, volume, and the session's activity profile are all reinforcing structure more than they are breaking it apart.

The main strain is coming from the move itself. Shorter-term and swing price pushes are the pieces leaning against the session, which leaves SPY looking supported, but not fully comfortable yet. For a first read, this is a support-led session that is still taking shape.
