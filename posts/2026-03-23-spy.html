Method notes

Strongest support source: Order-Flow Pressure. Strongest pressure source: Volatility Pulse.

  • Short Move: 1-bar log return, then tanh-normalized on a 64-bar rolling z-score.
  • Swing Move: 5-bar log return, then tanh-normalized on a 64-bar rolling z-score.
  • Volatility Pulse: 15-bar rolling volatility of short move, then tanh-normalized.
  • Volume Shock: Volume z-score vs 20-bar rolling mean/std, then tanh-normalized.
  • Order-Flow Pressure: 20-bar rolling sum of signed volume (OBV-like pressure), then tanh-normalized.
  • Money Flow: 14-bar Money Flow Index from typical price and volume, then tanh-normalized.
  • Drift: 20-bar linear slope of close, then tanh-normalized.
