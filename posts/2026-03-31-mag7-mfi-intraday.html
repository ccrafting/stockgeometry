MAG7: Support, Carry, and Money Have Broken Apart

Session date: 2026-03-31 | Session time (ET): 09:30 to 14:19

The Mag7 is no longer moving as a coherent leadership block. On the order-flow axis, support has narrowed almost entirely into Nvidia. On the trend axis, Amazon is carrying the clearest directional support, with Meta only slightly underneath it. On the money-flow axis, Nvidia is again the only supportive name, while Apple, Google, Microsoft, and Amazon are the heaviest strain sources. The important fact is not just that the group is weak. It is that the remaining support, the remaining carry, and the remaining money are no longer sitting in the same names.

That leaves the group badly fractured. Microsoft is negative on all three axes. Google and Tesla are negative on all three as well. Apple is close to that same condition, with the worst money-flow reading in the group. Nvidia is still being defended, but it is not carrying the cleanest directional shape. Amazon still has directional carry, but money is not confirming it. This is not a shared generals bid. It is a split leadership structure where support, direction, and money have come apart.
