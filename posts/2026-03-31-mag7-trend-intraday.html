MAG7: Support and Carry Have Broken Apart

Session date: 2026-03-31 | Session time (ET): 09:30 to 13:53

On the order-flow axis, support inside the Mag7 has narrowed almost entirely into Nvidia. Microsoft is the heaviest strain source there, with Google close behind, followed by Tesla and Amazon. Meta is also negative, and Apple is only barely less so. That object is not saying “tech is weak” in some vague way. It is saying supportive pressure is no longer shared across the group. On that axis, almost the entire complex is leaning the wrong way at once, with Nvidia as the one clear exception.

On the trend axis, the leadership changes. Amazon is the clearest source of directional support, and Meta is slightly supportive too. Microsoft, Apple, Tesla, Nvidia, and Google all lean negative on trend. That matters because it means the remaining directional carry is not sitting in the same name that is still attracting the strongest support pressure. Nvidia is still being sponsored, but it is not the cleanest directional leader. Amazon has the clearest carry, but it is not where the strongest supportive pressure is concentrated.

That split is the actual Mag7 message. The group does not have a common leader and it does not have a common failure mode. Nvidia is being defended. Amazon is carrying the best directional shape. Microsoft looks especially poor because it is the top strain source on order-flow and the top strain source on trend. Google and Tesla are weak on both axes as well. This is not a coherent generals bid. It is a fractured leadership structure where support, carry, and weakness have separated into different names.
