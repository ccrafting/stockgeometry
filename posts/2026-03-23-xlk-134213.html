XLK: Holding Together with Support

Session date: 2026-03-23 | Session time (ET): 09:30 to 15:59

XLK kept a constructive lean, but it never resolved into a clean, easy trend. Support stayed slightly ahead of pressure, led by Money Flow, while Volatility Pulse remained the clearest source of strain.

What matters is the quality of that support: it was real, but it was shared and somewhat rotating rather than resting on one dominant anchor. This read more like a sector holding together through a layered support field than one pressing higher in relaxed confidence.
